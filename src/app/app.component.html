The above graph plots the 'valence' of the songs in your recent listening history.
Tracks with high valence sound more positive (happy, cheerful, euphoric),
while tracks with low valence sound more negative (sad, depressed, angry).
Valence is computed through machine learning: an algorithm was trained on
a set of songs that were particularly happy or sad, and then resulting knowledge
was applied to assess the mood of other songs in the Spotify library.
